### Founder & Lead Systems Engineer at Skylantix.

I run a constellation of self-hosted services across servers nationwide.

### the path here

  Got here sideways. High school was deep in AP exams (for the love
  of it, not for college apps), choir, composing choral music. Picked
  up aviation and travel obsessions at the same time.

  Took a gap year after freshman year at UChicago. Visited 24 countries
  across all six inhabited continents in 2023 alone. Rewired how I think
  about most things.

  Came back, found Linux, went all in. Ditched the iPhone, MacBook, and
  Windows desktop. The infrastructure obsession started there. Composition
  fell by the wayside, but the creative problem-solving it taught me
  shows up everywhere in systems design.
